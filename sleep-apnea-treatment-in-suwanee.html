How long does it take to get fitted with a custom oral appliance for sleep apnea at your office?
The process usually takes about 2 to 3 weeks from your initial consultation to receiving your custom oral appliance. At your first visit, Dr. Shah will evaluate your symptoms and take digital impressions of your teeth. Your appliance is then custom-made at a dental lab, and you’ll return for a fitting and adjustment to ensure comfort and effectiveness.
Is Sugarloaf Signature Dentistry licensed to provide sleep apnea treatment, and is Dr. Shah specially trained for this service?
Yes, Dr. Shah is a licensed dentist in Georgia and has completed specialized training in dental sleep medicine. Our practice follows all state and federal regulations for sleep apnea treatment, and Dr. Shah is experienced in fitting and managing oral appliances for patients with mild to moderate obstructive sleep apnea.
What is the process for starting sleep apnea treatment at your Suwanee office?
To get started, schedule a consultation at our Suwanee location.
